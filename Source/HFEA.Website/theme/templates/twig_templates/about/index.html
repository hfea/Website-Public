Why we’re here

Our job is to ensure that everyone treated at a fertility clinic, and everyone conceived as a result of treatment, receives high quality care. We do this by:

  • licensing fertility clinics and human embryo research centres and ensuring they meet minimum standards. If they don’t, we have the power to shut them down if necessary.
  • collecting information from clinics which allows us to:
    • publish performance data for individual clinics and information on national trends
    • provide individuals with information about their donor and genetic siblings
  • providing guidance to clinics and making decisions on policies related to the fertility sector
  • considering the legal, ethical and scientific implications of new fertility techniques and research, advising the Government where appropriate.
